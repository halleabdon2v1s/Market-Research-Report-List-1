<p><p>The passenger car motor oil market is primarily divided into two types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. In contrast, synthetic oil is engineered through chemical processes, providing superior performance, better thermal stability, and enhanced protection against engine wear. This makes synthetic oil suitable for high-performance cars and extreme driving conditions, driving its growing popularity among consumers seeking efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
