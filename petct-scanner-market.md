<p><p>A PET/CT scanner is a medical imaging device that combines Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and Computed Tomography (CT) into a single unit, enabling enhanced diagnostic capabilities. By integrating metabolic and anatomical information, it provides comprehensive insights into various conditions, particularly cancer, neurological disorders, and cardiovascular diseases. This advanced technology allows for early detection, accurate staging of diseases, and monitoring of treatment response, making it invaluable in clinical settings.</p><p>The PET/CT scanner market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 11.7% during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the rising incidence of cancer globally, increasing awareness about early diagnosis, and technological advancements in imaging systems. Additionally, the aging population is contributing to a higher prevalence of chronic diseases, further fueling demand for PET/CT imaging. </p><p>Recent trends in the market include the development of hybrid imaging systems, the integration of Artificial Intelligence for enhanced image analysis, and improvements in scanner sensitivity and resolution. The growing adoption of molecular imaging methodologies in clinical practice is also notable, as it significantly aids in personalized medicine approaches. Overall, the PET/CT scanner market remains dynamic, with continuous innovations enhancing its role in modern diagnostics.</p></p>
