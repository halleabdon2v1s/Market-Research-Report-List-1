Type II and III anti-icers feature thicker viscosities, allowing them to provide longer-lasting protection against ice formation, with Type II being suitable for slower aircraft and Type III for faster jets. Type IV products offer similar protection as Type II, but with higher fluidity for use on faster aircraft.</p></p>
