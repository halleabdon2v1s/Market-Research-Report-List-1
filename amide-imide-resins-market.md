The market is primarily segmented into two types: linear amide-imide resins and branched amide-imide resins. Linear amide-imide resins are characterized by a straight-chain structure, offering superior mechanical properties and thermal resistance, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, branched amide-imide resins feature a branched molecular architecture, enhancing flexibility and processing characteristics, suitable for electronic and industrial applications.</p></p>
