<p><p>The breast cancer therapeutics drugs market comprises several key types. Mitotic inhibitors block cell division, preventing cancer cell proliferation. Anti-metabolites disrupt DNA synthesis, targeting rapidly dividing cells. Hormone receptor therapies block hormones like estrogen from fueling cancer growth. Aromatase inhibitors reduce estrogen production, useful in hormone-sensitive tumors. HER2 inhibitors target the HER2 protein overexpressed in some breast cancers, blocking signals that promote tumor growth. Together, these therapies provide tailored treatment options to improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
