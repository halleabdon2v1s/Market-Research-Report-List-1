<p><p>The Healthcare-associated Infectious Disease Diagnostics market encompasses various types of infections prevalent in clinical settings. Surgical Site Infections (SSIs) arise post-surgery and require specific microbial testing for effective management. Hospital-acquired Pneumonia (HAP) diagnostics involve respiratory specimen analysis. Bloodstream Infections (BSIs) necessitate blood cultures to identify pathogens, while Gastrointestinal Infections involve stool sample testing to detect pathogens affecting the digestive system. Each segment addresses unique diagnostic needs, enhancing patient care.</p></p>
