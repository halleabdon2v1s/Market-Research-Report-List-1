<p><p>The Hard Seal Butterfly Valves market can be divided into two main types: Flanged and Flangeless. Flanged butterfly valves feature external flanges that facilitate attachment to piping systems, providing a secure and robust connection. They are commonly used in high-pressure applications. In contrast, Flangeless butterfly valves lack these external fittings, allowing for a more compact design and easier installation in tight spaces. This type is ideal for low-pressure applications and lightweight installations, offering flexibility and reduced material costs.</p></p>
