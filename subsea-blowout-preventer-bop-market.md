<p><strong>The Subsea Blowout Preventer (BOP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Annular Blowout Preventer</li><li>Ram Blowout Preventer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Subsea Blowout Preventer (BOP) market includes devices designed to prevent uncontrolled releases of oil and gas from wells. Two main types are Annular Blowout Preventers and Ram Blowout Preventers. Annular BOPs provide a seal around the wellbore using a rubber element, accommodating various pipe sizes. In contrast, Ram BOPs utilize steel rams to close off the well, effectively sealing it regardless of pipe presence. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety and environmental protection during subsea drilling operations.</p></p>
