<p><strong>The Ceruloplasmin Determination Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nephelometry</li><li>Immunoturbidimetry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceruloplasmin Determination Kit market is segmented into two primary types: nephelometry and immunoturbidimetry. Nephelometry utilizes the scattering of light by particles in a solution to measure ceruloplasmin levels, offering high sensitivity and rapid results. Immunoturbidimetry, on the other hand, involves the formation of immune complexes that scatter light, allowing for quantification of ceruloplasmin. Both methods serve crucial roles in diagnosing and monitoring various health conditions, particularly those related to copper metabolism and liver function.</p></p>
