<p><p>The 5G RF inductors market consists of various types, each suited for specific applications. Wire wound inductors, known for their high current capacity and reliability, are ideal for power applications. Film inductors offer lower inductance values with minimal parasitic effects, making them suitable for high-frequency circuits. Multilayer inductors combine multiple layers of conductive and dielectric materials, providing compact designs with high inductance and excellent performance in RF applications. Together, these types support the diverse demands of 5G technology.</p></p>
