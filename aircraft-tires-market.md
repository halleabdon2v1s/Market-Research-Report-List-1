<p><p>The aircraft tires market primarily consists of radial and bias tires, each serving distinct applications. Radial tires feature a construction where plies run radially from the center, offering improved fuel efficiency and enhanced wear resistance, making them ideal for commercial aviation. Conversely, bias tires have plies that crisscross, providing a sturdy structure and greater load-carrying capacity, suitable for military and general aviation. Each type is designed to meet specific performance requirements, ensuring safety and reliability during flight operations.</p></p>
