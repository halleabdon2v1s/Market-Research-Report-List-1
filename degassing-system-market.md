<p><p>The degassing system market is divided into several types based on their operational mechanisms. Membrane degassing systems utilize semi-permeable membranes to remove gases from liquids efficiently. Vacuum degassing systems create a low-pressure environment, allowing dissolved gases to escape. Flash degassing systems rapidly reduce pressure to liberate gases from liquids instantly. Other systems may include innovative or hybrid technologies that combine characteristics of the aforementioned types, catering to specific industrial applications and requirements in gas removal processes.</p></p>
