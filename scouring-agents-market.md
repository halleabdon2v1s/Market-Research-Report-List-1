<p><p>The scouring agents market is segmented into three types: anionic, cationic, and nonionic scouring agents. Anionic scouring agents, typically negatively charged, are effective in removing dirt and hydrocarbons, often used in textile and laundry applications. Cationic scouring agents, with positive charges, are preferred for their antibacterial properties and are commonly used in personal care products. Nonionic scouring agents, which carry no charge, provide gentle cleaning action and are versatile across various industries, including textiles and household cleaning.</p></p>
