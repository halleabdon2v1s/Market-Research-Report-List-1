<p><ul><li>Dual Function Pressure Switch</li><li>HPCO</li><li>Fan</li><li>Compressor Cycling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive pressure switch market comprises several types, including dual function pressure switches, high-pressure cut-off switches (HPCO), fan switches, and compressor cycling switches. Dual function switches manage both pressure monitoring and control for various systems. HPCO switches activate at specific pressure thresholds to protect components from damage. Fan switches regulate engine cooling fans based on temperature, while compressor cycling switches control the operation of air conditioning compressors to maintain optimal performance and efficiency within automotive systems.</p></p>
