<p><p>The infectious disease testing device market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Molecular Diagnostic Tests** detect pathogens using techniques like PCR and genetic sequencing, offering high sensitivity and specificity for identifying infections at a genetic level.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Point-of-Care Testing (POCT)** provides rapid results at the time and place of patient care, enhancing timely diagnosis and treatment.</p><p>3. **Immunodiagnostic Tests** utilize antibody-antigen interactions to identify infections, commonly employed in serological assays for various diseases. Together, these technologies improve disease management and patient outcomes.</p></p>
