<p><p>The Crystalline Waterproofing Coating market is primarily divided into two types: Cement-based Permeable Crystalline Waterproof Coating and Water-based Permeable Crystalline Waterproof Coating. Cement-based coatings utilize cement as a key ingredient, forming a crystalline structure that fills pores and cracks, providing durable waterproofing. In contrast, Water-based coatings are easier to apply and environmentally friendly, using water as a solvent to deliver the crystalline properties. Both types enhance the durability and water resistance of structures, ensuring long-lasting protection.</p></p>
