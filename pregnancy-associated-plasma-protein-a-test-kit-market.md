<p><p>The Pregnancy-associated Plasma Protein A (PAPP-A) test kit market is primarily segmented into two main types: time-resolved immunofluorescence and chemiluminescence immunoassay. Time-resolved immunofluorescence utilizes fluorescent labels and time-gated detection for enhanced sensitivity and specificity in measuring PAPP-A levels. Conversely, chemiluminescence immunoassay employs chemical reactions to generate light, facilitating the quantification of PAPP-A with high precision. Both methods are essential for prenatal screening, aiding in the assessment of fetal health and potential complications during pregnancy.</p></p>
