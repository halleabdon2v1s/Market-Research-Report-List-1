<p><p>Electric Brake Systems (EBS) are crucial in modern vehicles, enhancing braking efficiency and safety. The market is primarily divided into two types: Disc EBS and Drum EBS. Disc EBS utilizes discs for superior heat dissipation and performance, making it ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, Drum EBS employs drum brakes, typically found in heavier vehicles, offering robust performance and cost-effectiveness. Both systems are integral for improving vehicle control, reducing stopping distances, and enhancing overall safety on the road.</p></p>
